  <context>
    The original Phase 3 design (from 03-CONTEXT.md and CAMP-02/UX-02) specified 4 fixed slots:
    - שבוע לפני — auto-sends 7 days before event at 09:00 (no date entry required)
    - יום לפני — auto-sends 1 day before event at 09:00 (no date entry required)
    - בוקר האירוע — user picks time, date auto-set to event day (minimal entry)
    - חצי שעה לפני — auto-sends at event_time minus 30 min (no entry required)

    What was actually built (Plan 03-04): 4 generic numbered slots (הודעה 1–4) with free-form date + time pickers. Michal enters a date manually per slot.

    UX-02 says: "הגדרת זמני שליחה בבחירה ויזואלית — לא בכתיבת תאריכים ידנית". The flexible design requires date entry per slot, arguably violating the "not manually" intent.

    The human-verify in Plan 03-04 was approved, but may have been evaluated in isolation without comparing against CAMP-02/UX-02 specification.

    CHOICE A — Keep flexible date pickers (current design):
    Pros: Already built and working. More flexibility — not constrained to event-relative offsets.
    Cons: Requires manual date entry per slot (4 dates per campaign). Does not auto-update if event date changes. Deviates from UX-02.

    CHOICE B — Restore fixed offset labels (original spec):
    Pros: Matches CAMP-02 and UX-02 exactly. Zero date entry — event date auto-computes all slot send_at values.
    Cons: Requires refactoring CampaignSheet slots section (~1-2 hours). Less flexibility — only 4 fixed offset patterns.
  </context>
